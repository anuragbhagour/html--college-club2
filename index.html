    The Horizon

    Discover the world of opportunities, connections and excitement right here on our campus. Explore this website to learn about oue various clubs, their upcoming events, and how you can get involved. Join us on this journey of discovery and personal growth as you find your niche in the horizon.

    Student clubs in our college

    Cultural club

    It is the place where students can showcase what they have got. It helps to discover their talent. Cultural events include competitions such as instrumental, singing, dance etc. Every year this club organizes activities such as Talent Hunt, Fresher's Party, Farewell, Fest and Alumni Meet.

    Technical club

    The club consists of self-motivated students who gather together to come up with some or the other new and innovative ideas and work on them. It aims to technically strengthen the students by integrating their skills in the various fields of Engineering & Technology, so as to cope up with the highly competitive environment.

    Social club

    Social clubs encourage interaction and conversation, which improve learning. Every activity the club conducts will inspire students to develop a new outlook on life. Students get the chance to develop and display their talents.

    Eco club

    Eco club seeks to promote environmental awareness at the grassroot level by creating and nurturing green consciousness among the students.

    Sports club

    The Sports club organizes Intra-Collegiate Sports events to provide leadership and recreation opportunities to the students and faculty in order to enhance their knowledge and skill in a specific sport of interest while at the same time retaining social, cognitive and physical experiences.

    ARTS club

    The ARTS club was established with the core idea of involving and developing students' passions and talents. The club emphasises on social skills, intellectual skills, organising skills, moral values, personality and character development of students.

    Drama club

    Drama Club is designed for students interested in learning more about the aspects of the theater. This includes learning- stage terminology, working on acting skills, set costumes/makeup design. Drama Club is open to all students.

    Photography club

    The objective of the photography club is to provide a platform for the interested students to share and exhibit their creativity, knowledge and passion for photography. The club conducts regular exhibitions, workshops and guest lectures to enhance aesthetic and critical skills in photography.
